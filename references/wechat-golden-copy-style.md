# WeChat Golden Copy Style

Use this reference when generating WeChat Official Account copy for Juhuo AI activities.

Goal: produce a mature, publication-ready campus community article, not an event notice, fact summary, or short outline.

## Golden Style Target

The article should feel like:

- a warm invitation from a campus AI community
- a practical event preview for students who may bring projects, skills, or curiosity
- a lightly playful but credible社群推文
- a complete article with scene, motivation, detail, and CTA

It should not feel like:

- a compressed activity plan
- a bureaucratic notice
- a corporate SaaS announcement
- a short abstract before HTML layout

## Default Length

Unless the user explicitly asks for a short version:

- normal event invitation: 1200-1800 Chinese characters
- rich plan with background, rules, guest/topic, agenda: 1500-2200 Chinese characters
- never deliver a 500-800 character WeChat summary as the default article

If the generated article is short because facts are missing, explain the missing facts and still write a full draft with honest placeholders.

## Required Article Shape

Use this shape by default, adapting headings to the event:

1. **Title hook**: concrete, inviting, and activity-specific.
2. **Opening line / subtitle**: time, scene, or slogan, such as "从一个想法，到一个作品。"
3. **导语**: 2-4 paragraphs. Explain why this event exists now and why students should care.
4. **Golden sentence**: one memorable shift or invitation.
5. **Core section 01**: what the main event is really about, written as a scene.
6. **Core section 02**: extra highlight, guest, special topic, roadshow, or practical value if present.
7. **Participant invitation**: project owners, builders/free agents, beginners; each gets a human invitation.
8. **Rules / participation notes**: friendly checklist, not strict review language.
9. **Agenda**: each segment has a short explanation, not just a time label.
10. **Time and location**: clear, concrete, with tentative facts preserved.
11. **Closing invitation**: warm, specific, and easy to act on.

## Mature Juhuo Voice

Prefer:

- "这周日，来现场：看 AI 如何从工具箱变成实战项目"
- "过去我们聊了很多 AI 工具，但这一次，我们想聊聊落地"
- "周日下午最核心的时间，属于那群正在做东西的人"
- "不是答辩，也不是正式评审"
- "大家直接提问、交流、互相支招"
- "如果你是项目主理人"
- "如果你是独行侠"
- "哪怕只是好奇，也欢迎来旁听"
- "现场氛围很轻松"

Avoid:

- "活动旨在"
- "进一步推进"
- "赋能生态"
- "搭建平台"
- "核心能力杠杆"
- "现正式启动"
- "为确保活动顺利开展"

## Information Density Requirements

A publishable WeChat article must include most public/adaptable facts from the fact table:

- event identity and theme
- organizer
- time
- location or location uncertainty
- registration method or placeholder
- background and reason for the event
- core segments
- target participants
- rules/limits if present
- agenda with public-facing explanations
- CTA

Do not collapse rich facts into one sentence per section.

## Human Expansion Patterns

For participant groups, write like this:

```markdown
如果你是项目主理人：来这里听听最真实的反馈，看看有没有更好的技术路径。你也可以直接说出项目现在最缺什么，是技术开发、产品设计，还是一起推进的队友。
```

For agenda items, write like this:

```markdown
模拟路演：项目组会用相对轻松的方式介绍自己的方向、进展和卡点，现场同学可以直接提问，也可以给出自己的技术或产品建议。
```

For rules, write like this:

```markdown
AI 相关项目优先：本期更希望看到 AI 已经进入真实场景的项目，哪怕还只是原型，也欢迎带来现场聊。
```

## Failure Patterns

Reject and rewrite if the WeChat copy looks like:

- `01 适合谁来` with one short bullet per group
- `02 活动信息` as the main content
- an agenda list with no explanation
- fewer than 1000 Chinese characters despite rich public facts
- no concrete scene, no hook, no golden sentence
- text that could be posted as a QQ notice rather than a WeChat article

## Golden Article Reminder

The article should make the reader feel:

"这不是又一个通知，而是一个我可以真的去现场认识项目、认识人、推进想法的下午。"
